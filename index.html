ABOUT ME

I received my B.Tech. in Computer Science & Engineering from Maharshi Dayanand University, India. I received my M.S. and Ph.D. in Computer Science from Wayne State University, Detroit.

Now I am a Postdoctoral Research Associate in Computer Science Research group at Oak Ridge National Laboratory.

My research interests include software energy efficiency, data analytics, and high performance computing.

In my free time, I like to click pictures.
